About Us

We are a team of undergraduates set out to make easily accessible napping spots a reality on campus. Our diverse academic background allows insights from multiple perspectives to the problem and also provides us with the skillset to successfully tackle it.

"When I am tired while attending classes or completing homework on campus, I want an accessible, comfortable and ideally private space to take a power nap, so that I can enhance my productivity and get my work done effectively."
